Accessibility & Inclusive Design

Vision Assist is built with accessibility at its core, ensuring that everyone can navigate the world independently and confidently.

Our Accessibility Commitment

At Vision Assist, we believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. Our app is designed from the ground up with accessibility as a core principle, not an afterthought. We work closely with the visually impaired community to ensure our solutions meet real-world needs.

Community-Driven Design

Every feature is developed in partnership with users who have vision disabilities

Universal Design Principles

Following WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and best practices for inclusive design

Continuous Improvement

Regular updates based on user feedback and accessibility testing

Accessibility Features

Screen Reader Compatible

Fully compatible with VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android) screen readers for complete audio navigation.

  • Semantic HTML structure
  • ARIA labels and descriptions
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Focus management

Keyboard Navigation

Complete keyboard accessibility for users who prefer or require keyboard-only navigation.

  • Tab order optimization
  • Keyboard shortcuts
  • Focus indicators
  • Skip links

High Contrast Mode

High contrast display options and customizable color schemes for users with low vision.

  • High contrast themes
  • Color customization
  • Text size adjustment
  • Focus highlighting

Voice Control

Complete voice control functionality for hands-free operation of all app features.

  • Voice commands
  • Voice navigation
  • Voice settings
  • Voice feedback

Haptic Feedback

Rich haptic feedback for users who are deaf or hard of hearing, providing tactile information.

  • Vibration patterns
  • Directional feedback
  • Alert notifications
  • Customizable intensity

Customizable Interface

Highly customizable interface that adapts to individual needs and preferences.

  • Layout customization
  • Font size options
  • Spacing adjustments
  • Personal preferences

Standards & Compliance

WCAG 2.1 AA Compliant

Meets Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 Level AA standards for web accessibility.

Mobile Accessibility

Follows iOS and Android accessibility guidelines for mobile app development.

International Standards

Complies with international accessibility standards including EN 301 549 and Section 508.

User Testing

Regular testing with users who have various types of disabilities to ensure real-world usability.
