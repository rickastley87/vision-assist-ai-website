AI Capabilities

People Detection

Identifies people in your vicinity, their approximate distance, and whether they're moving or stationary.

  • Person counting and tracking
  • Distance estimation
  • Movement detection

Object Recognition

Recognizes and describes common objects, furniture, vehicles, and environmental features.

  • Furniture identification
  • Vehicle detection
  • Sign and text reading

Obstacle Detection

Identifies potential hazards and obstacles that could impede safe navigation.

  • Stairs and steps
  • Uneven surfaces
  • Construction zones

Path Planning

Analyzes the environment to suggest safe and efficient navigation routes.

  • Clear path identification
  • Alternative routes
  • Turn-by-turn guidance
