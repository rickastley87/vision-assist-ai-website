Powerful Features for Independent Living

Discover all the features that make Vision Assist the most comprehensive navigation app for people with vision disabilities.

Core Features

Real-Time Vision Analysis

Advanced computer vision AI continuously analyzes your surroundings through your phone's camera, identifying objects, people, and obstacles with 99.9% accuracy.

  • Object detection and recognition
  • People counting and tracking
  • Obstacle identification
  • Text and sign reading

Audio Descriptions

Get detailed audio descriptions of your environment, including object locations, distances, and potential navigation paths in natural, conversational language.

  • Natural language descriptions
  • Distance and direction information
  • Customizable detail levels
  • Multi-language support

Smart Navigation

Receive turn-by-turn audio directions and obstacle warnings to navigate safely and confidently in any environment, indoors or outdoors.

  • Turn-by-turn directions
  • Obstacle warnings
  • Alternative route suggestions
  • Indoor and outdoor navigation

Accessibility First Design

Designed specifically for users with vision disabilities, with voice commands, haptic feedback, and intuitive audio interfaces.

  • Voice command control
  • Haptic feedback
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast mode

Advanced Features

Voice Commands

Control the app entirely through voice commands. Ask questions, request specific information, or adjust settings hands-free.

Low Light Mode

Enhanced vision processing for low-light conditions, ensuring reliable navigation even in dimly lit environments.

Location Awareness

GPS integration provides context-aware descriptions and navigation assistance based on your current location.

Customizable Settings

Personalize your experience with adjustable settings for audio speed, detail level, and notification preferences.

Navigation History

Keep track of frequently visited locations and create custom routes for your daily routines.

Emergency Assistance

Quick access to emergency features and the ability to share your location with trusted contacts when needed.

Accessibility Features

Designed for Everyone

Vision Assist is built with accessibility at its core, ensuring that people with various levels of vision impairment can use the app effectively and independently.

Complete Blindness Support

Full audio interface with no visual elements required

Low Vision Support

High contrast mode and large text options for partial sight

Hearing Impaired Support

Visual indicators and haptic feedback for audio alternatives

Motor Impairment Support

Voice-only control and minimal touch interaction required
